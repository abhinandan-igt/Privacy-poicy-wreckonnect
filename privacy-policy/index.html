Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 18, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Wreckonnect handles your information when you use the Wreckonnect mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to enable core functionality:

  • Personal Information: Your name, phone number, email address, and (communication history).
  • Vehicle Part Requests: Details such as car model, required parts, and specifications.
  • Location Information: Only if location is enabled for delivery purposes.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and logs for crash reporting.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Facilitate the request and quote process between customers and suppliers.
  • Enable clear communication and order tracking.
  • Improve our app’s functionality and reliability.
  • Support user inquiries and manage account-related requests.

3. Payment Methods

Wreckonnect offers three distinct payment methods to accommodate a variety of user preferences:

  • Account (Offline): A direct payment arrangement between customers and suppliers. Payment details are shared privately and handled outside the app via bank transfers or cash.
  • Capricorn (Offline): A specialized offline payment method used by selected suppliers and customers based on pre-established agreements.
  • Stripe (Online): For users who prefer secure digital payments, Wreckonnect integrates with Stripe. All transactions are encrypted and processed through Stripe’s PCI-compliant system. No card or financial information is stored on our servers.

These options ensure flexibility, allowing users to choose the payment method that best suits their needs while maintaining transparency and security.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. We only share your data:

  • With suppliers—to process your vehicle part requests.
  • With trusted service providers—to operate and maintain the app.
  • When required by law or to protect rights and safety.

5. Data Security

We use standard security practices to protect your data. While no method is 100% secure, we take all reasonable steps to safeguard your information.

6. Your Rights

You may contact us anytime to request access to or deletion of your personal data. We honor all lawful data access requests promptly.

7. Children's Privacy

Wreckonnect is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed. Any changes will be reflected here with an updated effective date.
